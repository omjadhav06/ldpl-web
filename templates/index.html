Our History

Established on March 16, 1963, Lokvikas has grown into a leading dairy cooperative. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure includes:

  • Modern dairy plant with 17 lakh liters per day capacity
  • Satellite dairy facility
  • Four chilling centers with combined capacity of 8 lakh liters per day
