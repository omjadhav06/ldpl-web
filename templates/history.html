Our History

Lokvikas Dairy Private Limited (LDPL) was incorporated on March 19, 2009, in Solapur, Maharashtra, India. From its inception, the company focused on establishing itself as a key player in the dairy industry by manufacturing high-quality dairy products. In its early years, LDPL concentrated on building a strong supply chain, sourcing fresh milk from local farmers, and ensuring strict quality control measures. By 2015, the company had expanded its production capacity and diversified its product offerings.
